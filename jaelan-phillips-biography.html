With the intention of playing football for the Hurricanes, Phillips moved to the University of Miami’s Frost School of Music in 2019.

Due to NCAA transfer regulations, he was unable to participate in the 2019 season. However, he did start in 2020 and ended with 45 tackles, 8 sacks, and 1 interception.

The Miami Dolphins selected Phillips in the first round of the 2021 NFL Draft (18th overall). On June 9, 2021, he agreed to a $14 million, four-year rookie contract.

In 2021, Phillips made appearances in all 17 games as a rookie, starting four of them. In all, he recorded 42 tackles (23 solo), 8.5 sacks, and one pass defensed.
