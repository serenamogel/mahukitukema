Best Dairy Free Ice Cream, Ranked

June 2024 · 9 minute read

The best vegan ice cream is rich, creamy, and extremely scoopable. It doesn’t compromise decadence just because it’s made with dairy free ingredients. The flavors should be vibrant and harmonious, on par with an artisanal scoop from your favorite local ice cream parlor—seriously, if it’s anything less than sublime, we’re simply not recommending it.

Vegan ice cream has come such a long way over the years. Our theory? There’s been an unseen alliance forming between animal rights activists and sensitive tummy girlies since the early 2010s, and it’s finally paying dividends. So going into our second taste test of the best vegan ice cream, we predicted the Sporked freezer would quickly fill up with new non-dairy brands and flavors we hadn’t tried before—and we were right. Whether you’re searching for the best vegan vanilla ice cream, the best dairy free chocolate ice cream, or anything and everything in between, grab your little plastic sample spoons and dig into this list of the best dairy free ice cream. 

Best Fancy

365 Berry Chantilly Cake Almondmilk Non-Dairy Frozen DessertAmazon

If you’re a fruity, light, vanilla-y dessert person, grab a pint of this. The 365 by Whole Foods Berry Chantilly Cake Almondmilk Non-Dairy Frozen Dessert is like the most beautiful bakery cake you’ve ever tasted in ice cream form. I don’t even like fruity desserts (chocolate or death, imo), but I thought this was spectacular. It tastes exactly like berry chantilly cake! And even though it’s cake themed, it’s gluten-free! This is also some of the best vegan ice cream if you prefer an almond milk base to oat or coconut milk. —Jordan Myrick

Best Unique

Planet Oat Blueberry Oat Crumble Oatmilk Non-Dairy Frozen DessertInstacart

Planet Oat Blueberry Oat Crumble Oatmilk Non-Dairy Frozen Dessert is such a nice surprise! I’m not even that into fruit and I was obsessed with this ice cream. There are big pieces of oat streusel in the mix that make this taste like an actual blueberry crumble. The oat milk base pairs well with the flavor of the crumble—you get more than just the flavor of oats. I ended up bringing this pint of ice cream home and snacking on it for the rest of the week. —Jordan Myrick

Best Elevated

WildGood Caramelized Fig Frozen DessertInstacart

WildGood Caramelized Fig Frozen Dessert tastes exactly like fresh figs sauteed with brown sugar and butter poured over creamy vanilla ice cream. It’s such an elevated ice cream flavor that makes you feel sophisticated when you eat it. It might not be for everyone because the texture is a little chewy, much like a fig. But I love figs and I loved this vegan ice cream. This is the best dairy free ice cream if you’re in the mood for something unconventional. —Jordan Myrick

Best Cookies & Cream

So Delicious Dairy Free Chocolate Cookies ‘n’ Cream Frozen DessertTarget

So Delicious makes some of the best vegan ice cream out there (dairy-based brands included). This Chocolate Cookies ‘n’ Cream flavor is so perfectly chocolatey. The cookies are soft but not too soft. The chocolatey base makes me wonder why I haven’t been dipping my Oreos in chocolate milk instead of regular milk my entire life. This ice cream is not only a delicious dessert but an important reminder that we should all be consuming more chocolate milk. —Jordan Myrick

Best Strawberry

Mauna Loa Strawberry Guava Macadamia Milk Frozen DessertSprouts

This strawberry ice cream from Mauna Loa really surprised us. First, you have to look past what you may expect from a traditional strawberry ice cream. While it’s a great choice for strawberry lovers, the notes of guava and citrus give it a tropical edge. The texture is more icy than creamy, like a sorbet. It’s super addictive—in large part thanks to the unique blend of fresh fruit flavors. It may be a little unconventional, but honestly? It would be a crime not to name this the best vegan strawberry ice cream of the bunch. It’s truly sublime. —Ariana Losch

Best Coffee

Gelato Boy Dairy Free Coffee Fudge Swirl GelatoSprouts

Gelato Boy, where have you been all of my life? This pint of coffee fudge swirl ice cream is complex. It tastes like a traditional coffee ice cream, but with a rich chocolate undertone hidden in that hypnotic fudge swirl. The oat milk and coconut milk pair beautifully, and a subtle hint of coconut flavor adds even more depth to this non-dairy ice cream. If you’re searching for the best vegan coffee ice cream—or if an oat milk mocha latte is your go-to Starbucks drink—this is the dairy free ice cream for you. —Ariana Losch

Best Peanut Butter

So Delicious Dairy Free Peanut Butter Brownie Cashewmilk Frozen DessertInstacart

First things first, I don’t know what freaky food science is going on with So Delicious ice cream, but despite having a cashew milk and coconut base, neither of those flavors are present in this vegan ice cream. It’s all smooth, creamy, lightly sweet ice cream, dreamy peanut butter (that PB flavor is so good, y’all), and thick hunks of chocolate brownie. And look, full transparency, the team was divided on the brownie chunks. Some thought they were too grainy, others didn’t mind the taste or texture. But the standout flavor here—and the reason it’s one of the best vegan ice creams we tried—is that creamy peanut butter. If you’re a PB lover, buy this. —Ariana Losch

Best Brownie

Van Leeuwen Peanut Butter Brownie Honeycomb Non-Dairy Frozen DessertInstacart

I’m going to be so real with you: I wish I could rank this higher. Van Leeuwen has a real talent for making smooth, creamy, super scoopable vegan ice cream. One taste of their Peanut Butter Brownie Honeycomb frozen dessert will instantly brighten your day. It carries a lovely note of fresh-from-the-comb honey that lightly sweetens the mild peanut butter and brownie flavor blend. But there are some seriously hot contenders for the best vegan ice cream on this list, and if this one falls short of anything, it’s an identity. Is it the best vegan peanut butter ice cream? The best honeycomb? We settled on the best vegan brownie ice cream, because honestly, the brownie hunks have a nice texture and light cocoa flavor (and were much better than others we tried). —Ariana Losch

Best Mix-Ins

So Delicious Dairy Free Salted Caramel Cluster Frozen DessertTarget

So Delicious makes such good vegan ice cream products and I recommend trying every single one of them. Their cashew milk-based Salted Caramel Cluster tops my list, though. The light coconut flavor pairs wonderfully with the caramel and cashew. But it’s not so coconutty that it overpowers the other flavors in the mix. It’s sweet, but not too sweet. It’s crazy creamy. This is such a balanced vegan ice cream and a real palate pleaser. Get this and thank me later! —Jordan Myrick

Best Vanilla

Craig’s Non-Dairy Killa’ Vanilla Frozen DessertInstacart

Craig’s is an L.A.-based vegan ice cream shop that’s churning out some of the best dairy free ice cream you’ll find in grocery stores nationwide. Their Killa’ Vanilla flavor did, in fact, come in with the kill, unseating our previous pick for “best vegan vanilla ice cream.” It excelled in every way—its texture is creamy, almost buttery, the flavor is bold, and it even has aesthetic appeal, thanks to specks of vanilla bean. If you’re looking for something gourmet to ladle with hot fudge or serve with a slice of pie, this is the best vegan ice cream for you. —Ariana Losch

Best Chocolate

Cado Deep Dark Chocolate Non-Dairy Avocado Frozen DessertInstacart

This chocolate ice cream is truly deep and dark, just like the name promises. It’s smooth, creamy, and rich. It tastes like it’s made with full-fat dairy and there’s no weird aftertaste. It’s just 100% pure chocolate deliciousness. Some of Cado’s other flavors were not good, but they knocked it out of the park with Deep Dark Chocolate. Who knew chocolate avocado ice cream could be so good? If you don’t do dairy, this is the best vegan chocolate ice cream you can buy. —Jordan Myrick

Runner Up

Trader Joe’s Black Tea and Boba Coconut Non-Dairy Frozen Dessert

We tasted and reviewed this dairy free ice cream when it hit TJ’s frozen aisle in 2022, and we were all blown away by it. In fact, it wasn’t just the best vegan ice cream we tasted that year—it was the best new ice cream, period.  Now I buy this Black Tea and Boba Coconut Non-Dairy Frozen Dessert every time I go to Trader Joe’s. The ice cream tastes exactly like milk tea. The boba is somehow chewy and not hard or frozen. There’s some kind of sticky, caramel-y swirl. All of these components come together harmoniously to make one of the best dairy free ice creams I’ve ever tasted. Go buy this! —Jordan Myrick

Best of the Best

Ben & Jerry’s Phish Food Non-Dairy Frozen DessertInstacart

If you’re looking for a vegan ice cream that could fool even the biggest dairy lovers, look no further than Ben & Jerry’s Non-Dairy Phish Food Frozen Dessert. This tastes so shockingly similar to Ben & Jerry’s regular Phish Food that I was shaken to my core. It’s creamy and chocolatey and there are plenty of mix-ins. In all honesty, this ice cream has no flaws and I wouldn’t change a thing. Good job, Ben and Jerry! You made the best vegan ice cream of them all. —Jordan Myrick
