Gunna | Career

Gunna was introduced to Young Thug by a mutual friend Keith “King,” who died in December 2015.

He later featured with rappers Travis Scott and Gucci mane on Young Thug’s song. Gunna released his debut mixtape in October 2016, Drip Season, through YSL Records.

He released his second mixtape on May 11, 2017. The mixtape featured numerous well-known artists such as Playboi Carti and Offset.

That was just the beginning. Gunna later released 2018: Drip Season 3 and Drip Harder, 2019: Drip or Drown 2, and 2020: Wunna. These mixtapes skyrocketed his fame and fortune.
