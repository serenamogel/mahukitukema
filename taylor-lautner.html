Taylor Daniel Lautner is an American actor and model. He is mainly known for starring as Shapeshifter Jacob Black in ‘The Twilight Saga’ film series. In the film series, his performance was undeniable as a larger audience appreciated him for giving a fantabulous performance.

Taylor Lautner is known for his brilliance and admiring acting prowess for which he always gets appreciation from everyone including his die-hard fans and the viewers.

Career

Taylor Lautner started his career with small roles in comedy series, like; The Bernie Mac Show’ (2003), ‘My Wife and Kids'(2004), and many others.

Apart from acting in films, telefilms, television series, Lautner also lent his voice to the characters in TV series including ‘What’s New, Scooby-Doo’, (2005), ‘Danny Phantom’ (2005), and many more.

He has acted in the film named ‘Cheaper by the Dozen 2’ in 2005. Later, Taylor Lautner acted in ‘The Adventures of Sharkboy and Lavagirl in 3D as well in which he gave wonderful performances.

In 2011, he acted in the action film titled ‘Abduction’. From 2014 to 2018, the actor was seen in the BBC sitcom named ‘Cukoo’. In between, he gave another winning performance in the second season of ‘Fox Black Comedy’ series named ‘Scream Queens’.

However, the actor Taylor Lautner has built his broad career in which he acted in many hit projects for which he got huge acclaim.

Achievements

Here you will be amazed to know that Taylor Lautner has won numerous awards from nominations including Young Artist Award, Scream Awards, MTV Movie Awards, and many others that are on the list.
