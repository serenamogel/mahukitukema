Best Microwave Popcorn [Updated Taste Test!]

February 2024 · 8 minute read

The best microwave popcorn is exceptionally buttery, and salty, and the kernels are sweet, earthy, and have a distinct toasted corn flavor. Fake butter is welcome, we almost prefer its taste, but the best microwavable popcorn comes at you hard with either fatty, salty flavors or a pure, natural taste. So what is the best microwave popcorn that absolutely popped off? Read on to find out. 

We get hype for movie theater popcorn. “Where I’m from, when you go to the movie theater, you are there to watch Transformers 17: Age of the Quadrupeds, and walk out of it absolutely sick to your stomach from eating an entire tub of popcorn drowned in butter,” says Sporked contributor Danny Palumbo.“That’s the American way.”

But sometimes you want popcorn at home. What makes for the best microwave popcorn? Well, here’s what we looked for in this taste test: More than anything, we wanted the package to deliver on its promise. If it says “extra butter,” then we’re assuming there’s going to be a ton of butter. If it says “sea salt,” then we expect a seasoned bag of popcorn with a subtle, not iodized, salt taste. If it says “organic,” then we want fluffy, tasty kernels that don’t feel too dry. And if it says “movie theater” on it, well, we expect it to be the perfect alchemy of salt and butter.

When it comes to the best microwave popcorn, we either want something that’s organic, textured, and just a tad sweet, or something completely drenched in butter and salt. Here at Sporked, we want food to be healthy or we want it to kill us. “Anything in the middle sucks,” says senior staff writer Jordan Myrick.

So peep the list and find the best microwave popcorn brand for you.

If you like plain popcorn, Quinn is the best microwave popcorn for you. That said, the seasoning and the butter for this popcorn come in little packets on the side, so you’re really in the driver’s seat when it comes to dictating the amount of salt and butter here. We tried Quinn plain, and, man, it was really good. It has a clean and fresh corn flavor to it, and the kernels aren’t rubbery so they don’t get stuck in your teeth. It’s just fluffy and airy, making it highly snackable. There is something really gratifying about plain popcorn that allows you to savor each bite. It’s probably the more dignified way to eat, instead of shoveling loads of butter soaked, salt packed popcorn into your mouth like Jordan and I do. Quinn is tasty and simple.
