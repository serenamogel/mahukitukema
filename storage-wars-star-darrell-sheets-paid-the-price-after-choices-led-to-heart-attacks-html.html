Storage Wars star Darrell Sheets paid the price after choices led to heart attacks

March 2024 · 3 minute read
Spread the love

A High-Risk, High-Reward Journey

Storage Wars, a TV show full of surprises, rewards, and risks, introduced us to Darrell “The Gambler” Sheets back in 20Over the years, this 64-year-old TV star has amassed millions from incredible finds worth more than $300,000. Today, he stands as one of the show’s most successful personalities.

A Surprising Retirement and a Welcome Return

After Season 14, Darrell Sheets surprised fans by retiring from Storage Wars. The reason behind this decision was primarily linked to a significant health scare he experienced in 201However, good news awaits fans as Storage Wars is set to make a comeback in 2023.

 Darrell Sheets’ Health Wake-Up Call

In 2019, when the internet was abuzz with the 10-year challenge, Darrell Sheets took part in a different manner. His participation aimed to shed light on the importance of a healthy lifestyle. He posted a side-by-side picture of himself from 2013, highlighting the stark contrast to his health at the time.

The Shocking Revelation

In a March 2019 Instagram post, Darrell shared a shocking revelation. He had suffered not one but two consecutive heart attacks. His caption read, “For those of you that don’t know, I had two back-to-back heart attacks. Little did I know how much my life was going to change.”

 Life-Altering Surgery

Following his heart attacks, Darrell Sheets underwent surgery. Two stents were inserted as one of his heart valves was 95% blocked, and the other was 80% blocked. The surgery was critical for his survival.

A New Reality

Post-surgery, Darrell’s life took a different turn. He revealed, “My heart is only 25 to 30% functioning, and I have a machine now attached to me 24/7, and 27 pills every day of my life.” It was a significant shift from his previous lifestyle.

 The Price of Neglecting Health

Darrell Sheets acknowledged his role in his health issues, admitting, “I was a closet diabetic that would eat whatever I wanted and masked it with diabetic pills. I’m paying the price. I beg all of my friends, fans, and family to really pay attention to your health.”

A Fresh Start

Despite the challenges, Darrell made a remarkable recovery and is now enjoying life to the fullest with his partner, Kimber Wuerfel. After divorcing Romney Snyder in 2016, he found happiness with Kimber, as evidenced by their social media updates since 2020.

 Storage Wars Season 15 Confirmation

While A&E has yet to announce the air date for Season 15 of Storage Wars, Darrell Sheets and other stars have dropped hints about new episodes. Darrell, in particular, took to Instagram to reveal that the new series is expected to debut in April 2023.

 Filming Already Underway

Although May 2022 is just around the corner and no official announcement has been made, the excitement is building. Casey Nezhoda inadvertently let slip that filming for the new season had already commenced in May 2022.

Darrell Sheets’ journey on and off the Storage Wars set has been nothing short of extraordinary. From health challenges to a renewed lease on life, he’s shown that it’s never too late to make positive changes. Fans eagerly await his return to the world of storage auctions, ready for more surprises and gambles.
