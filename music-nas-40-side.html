MUSIC: Nas 40 Side

July 2024 · 1 minute read

Prolific American rapper and hip hop recording artiste, Nas lashes out a new impressive tune captioned; “40 Side” off his just released album, “King’s Disease II”.

“King’s Disease II” is a continuation of the exploration which began with his first project, “King’s Disease”. The body of works is a fifteen track project with features from top tier American musicians.

The first “King’s Disease” which was his 13th studio album bagged an award for Best Rap Album at the 2021 Grammy Awards. The second ‘King’s Disease’ album is executive produced by Nas and Hit-Boy, who as well features on the song ‘Composure’.

Nas is regarded as one of the greatest and most influential rappers of all time. He’s an entrepreneur through his own record label, he also serves as associate publisher of Mass Appeal magazine and the co-founder of Mass Appeal Records.
